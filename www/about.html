GTagHD

Iowa State/Mayo Clinic Alliance for Genome Engineering

Homology directed gene targeting allows for the integration of exogenous DNA into the genome with base pair and reading frame specificity. However, design and assembly of homologous targeting vectors for a gene of interest can be time consuming.

Our modular plasmid series, pGTag, and homology design web tool, GTagHD, enable researchers to quickly design, build, and integrate a variety of cargos into a gene of interest. Golden Gate cloning of annealed oligonucleotide homology arms allows efficient, inexpensive vector assembly.

Follow the prompts under the “Submit Single Job” tab to have GTagHD design and output ready-to-order ssDNA oligonucleotides to be used in our GTag series gene targeting protocol.
